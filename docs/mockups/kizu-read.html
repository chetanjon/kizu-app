why this lands

reveal, don’t describe

“you are slow mornings and frank ocean.” (you already know this — useless)
“you queue ambitious films and finish comfort ones.” (a true thing you’d never have said out loud)

1 · Revealed > stated. People are wrong about their own taste. The gold is the gap between what you say you love and what you actually finish, replay, return to. kizu has both — most apps only have one.

2 · The pattern you can’t self-see. Timing, repetition, what you reach for when. You can’t observe your own habits from the inside; the data can.

3 · A little provocation. One line that reframes you (“you’re kinder to yourself than you think”). Mild surprise = it feels seen, not flattered.

4 · Show the evidence. “noticed from: queued vs finished, what you reach for after 10pm.” Receipts make it true, not a horoscope.
