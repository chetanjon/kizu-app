⑤ The Merge

One feed, two physics. Up top: your group — finite, trusted, ends in “caught up.” That line isn’t a wall — it’s a threshold into Kizu Curate.

Below it: an infinite river of real people’s taste — a face, a name, where you met them, their words, their pick. Organized by moment (“sunday melancholy”), not genre. Endless, but every item is hand-curated by a human, so it’s never slop.

You’re never bored, never see “nothing here” — but it’s not doomscroll, because it’s all good taste from real people. You leave because life called, not because it went empty.
